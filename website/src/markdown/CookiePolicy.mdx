# Cookie Policy for NextMunday

**Last Updated:** June 3, 2025

NextMunday ("we," "us," or "our") uses cookies and similar technologies to enhance your experience on our website, nextmunday.com. This Cookie Policy explains what cookies are, how we use them, and your choices regarding their use.

## What are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are placed on your computer or mobile device when you visit a website. They are widely used to make websites work more efficiently, as well as to provide information to the owners of the site. Cookies can remember your preferences, track your Browse activity, and help deliver a more personalized experience.

## How We Use Cookies

We use cookies for a variety of purposes, including:

* **Essential / Strictly Necessary Cookies:** These cookies are essential for the operation of our website and enable you to navigate around it and use its features. Without these cookies, services like logging into secure areas or using shopping carts cannot be provided.
    * **Related to Google Firebase:** Firebase uses certain cookies and local storage to enable core functionalities such as authentication (e.g., keeping you logged in) and storing basic session information. These are fundamental for the proper functioning of user accounts and features powered by Firebase.

* **Analytical / Performance Cookies:** These cookies collect information about how you use our website, such as which pages you visit most often, and if you encounter any error messages. This helps us to improve the way our website works.
    * **Related to Google Analytics:** We use Google Analytics to understand how visitors engage with our website. Google Analytics collects information anonymously and reports website trends without identifying individual visitors. This data helps us analyze website traffic, understand user behavior, and improve our content and user experience. Google Analytics uses its own cookies to track visitor interactions.

* **Functionality Cookies:** These cookies allow our website to remember choices you make (such as your username, language, or the region you are in) and provide enhanced, more personal features. For example, they may be used to remember changes you have made to text size, fonts, and other parts of web pages that you can customize.

## Third-Party Cookies

As mentioned above, we use services provided by Google, specifically Google Firebase and Google Analytics, which set their own cookies. These are considered third-party cookies.

* **Google Firebase:** Firebase may set cookies for functionalities like user authentication and session management. You can find more information about Google's cookie practices at [https://policies.google.com/technologies/cookies](https://policies.google.com/technologies/cookies).
* **Google Analytics:** Google Analytics uses cookies to collect anonymous information about your website usage. You can learn more about Google Analytics' privacy practices at [https://policies.google.com/privacy](https://policies.google.com/privacy) and opt-out of Google Analytics tracking by installing the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on: [https://tools.google.com/dlpage/gaoptout](https://tools.google.com/dlpage/gaoptout).

## Your Choices Regarding Cookies

You have the right to decide whether to accept or reject cookies. You can exercise your cookie preferences by:

* **Browser Settings:** Most web browsers allow you to control cookies through their settings. You can usually find these settings in the "options" or "preferences" menu of your browser. Please note that if you choose to block or delete cookies, this may affect the functionality of our website and other websites you visit.
* **Cookie Consent Tool (if applicable):** If we implement a cookie consent banner or tool, you can use it to manage your cookie preferences directly on our website.
* **Third-Party Opt-Outs:** As mentioned above, you can use the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on to prevent your data from being used by Google Analytics.

For more information about how to manage cookies, you can visit [www.allaboutcookies.org](http://www.allaboutcookies.org/).
